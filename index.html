Game Description

Candy Quest is a vibrant match-3 puzzle adventure where you swipe, match, and crush candies through magical levels with fun boosters and charming characters — perfect for both casual players and puzzle pros.

Gallery

How to Play

  • Swipe to Match

    Connect 3+ candies of the same color

  • Complete Goals

    Collect targets before running out of moves

  • Use Boosters

    Trigger candy bombs and rainbow blasts

  • Unlock Worlds

    Progress through magical candy lands

Key Features

  • Hundreds of colorful match-3 levels

  • Sweet boosters and special candy combos

  • Fun and casual gameplay

  • Offline mode available

  • Adorable candy-themed graphics
